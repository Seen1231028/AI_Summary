### Bayes‚Äô Rule
$$P(a \mid b) = \frac{P(b \mid a)P(a)}{P(b)}$$
### We know:
$$P(on-315 \mid crash) = 0.8$$
$$P(crash) = 0.1 $$
$$P(on-315) = 0.7$$
### ‡∏ß‡∏¥‡∏ò‡∏µ‡∏ó‡∏≥
$$P(crash \mid on-315) = \frac{P(on-315 \mid crash)P(crash)}{P(on-315)}$$
### ‡πÅ‡∏ó‡∏ô‡∏Ñ‡πà‡∏≤
$$\begin{align*}
P(\text{crash} \mid \text{on-315}) 
&= \frac{P(0.8 * 0.1)}{P(0.7)} \\
&= \frac{0.8}{0.7} \\
& \approx 0.114
\end{align*}$$
